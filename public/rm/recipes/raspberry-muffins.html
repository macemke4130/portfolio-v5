Raspberry Muffins

https://preppykitchen.com/raspberry-muffins/

Equipment

  • Muffin Tin
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Medium Mixing Bowl

Preparation

Preheat oven to 400

Ingredients

Large Mixing Bowl

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Medium Mixing Bowl

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoon melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Individual containers

  • 16 oz fresh raspberries

    Instructions

    1. In large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Make sure baking powder is broken up.
    2. In medium mixing bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, oil, melted butter, vanilla.
    3. Fold the egg mixture into the flour mixture, stirring just until combined.
    4. Once combined, add the raspberries.
    5. Divide the batter amongst the muffin tin.
    6. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes.
