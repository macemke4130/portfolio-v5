Baked Penne

https://themodernproper.com/baked-ziti

Preparation

  • Preheat Oven: 375F
  • Boil salted water in sauce pot

Equipment

  • Sauce Pot
  • Large Stock Pot
  • Aluminum foil

Ingredients

Prepared Ingredients

  • Dice Yellow Onion
  • Chop 6 garlic cloves
  • Thinly slice 8oz of fresh mozzarella

Measured Ingredients

  • 1 pound penne or ziti pasta
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 24oz marinara sauce jar
  • 15oz tomoato sauce can
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated or shredded parmesan

Instructions

Pasta

  • Cook penne to al dente. Drain.

Stock Pot

Over medium high heat, add:

  • Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Stir and cook for about 8 minutes. Drain excess fat from stock pot.

Add:

  • Marinara sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Heavy cream
  • Cooked penne
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella

Mix. Transfer mixture to baking dish.

Baking Dish

Top with:

  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  1. Cover baking dish with foil. Bake 20 minutes.
  2. Discard foil. Add parmesan.
  3. Bake for 5 minutes uncovered until the sauce is bubbly.
