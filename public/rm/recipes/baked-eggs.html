Baked Eggs

The Feed Zone Cook Book

Preparation

  • Preheat Oven: 350F

Equipment

  • 12 Muffin Tin

Ingredients

  • 12 Eggs
  • Milk or Cream
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Salt

Instructions

  • Crack 1 egg into each muffin tin
  • Add 1/2 Teaspoon to each muffin tin
  • Scramble with plastic fork
  • Bake for 7 minutes then rotate tray 180
  • Bake for 8 more minutes
  • Remove from oven
  • Add 1 teaspoon of parmesean cheese to each egg
  • Add salt to taste
