Veggie Thai Red Curry

https://cookieandkate.com/thai-red-curry-recipe/#tasty-recipes-23970-jump-target

Equipment

  • Large Pot
  • Large Pan
  • Medium mixing bowl
  • Large mixing bowl
  • 2 Demitasse
  • Small bowl

Preparation

Cook the rice first.

Ingredients

Prepared Ingredients

Large Pot

  • 1.25 cups of jasmine or white rice - cooked

Medium Mixing Bowl

  • 1 red bell pepper - 2 inch long strips
  • 1 yellow bell pepper - 2 inch long strips
  • 3 carrots - peeled and coined

Demitasse

  • 2 garlic cloves - minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger - finely grated

Small Bowl

  • 1 small white onion - diced

Measured Ingredients

Large Mixing Bowl

  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 0.5 cup water
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1.5 teaspoons brown sugar

Demitasse

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar or lime juice

Individual containers

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 4 oz tai curry paste

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Warm the large pan over medium heat. Once it's hot, add the oil.
  3. Add the onion and a sprinkle of salt. Cooked about 5 minutes, stirring often.
  4. Add the ginger and garlic and cook about 30 seconds, while stirring continuously.
  5. Add the bell peppers and carrots. Cook until the bell peppers are fork-tender, 3 to 5 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the curry paste and cook, stirring often, for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the coconut milk, water, kale and sugar, stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat. Maintain a gentle simmer and cook until the peppers, carrots and kale have softened to your liking, about 5 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Remove the pot from the heat and season with tamari and rice vinegar.
