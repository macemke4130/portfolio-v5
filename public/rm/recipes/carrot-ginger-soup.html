Carrot Ginger Soup

https://www.loveandlemons.com/carrot-ginger-soup/

Equipment

  • Stock Pot
  • Blender
  • Small mixing bowl

Ingredients

Prepared Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Carrots - Peeled and chopped
  • Yellow Onion - Diced
  • 3 Garlic Cloves - Smashed

Measured Ingredients

Small Mixing Bowl

  • 1 Teaspoon / Knub of ginger - Microplaned
  • 3 Cups Veggie Broth
  • 1 Tablespoon Apple Cider Vinegar

Separate Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon Maple Syrup
  • 1 Teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in the stock pot
  2. Add the onions, salt and pepper and cook for 8 minues. Stir occasionally.
  3. Add the smashed garlic cloves and carrots. Cook for 8 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  4. Add contents of mixing bowl.
  5. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a blender.
  7. Add maple syrup. Blend until smooth.
