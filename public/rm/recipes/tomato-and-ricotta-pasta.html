Tomato and Ricotta Pasta

https://theclevermeal.com/one-pot-tomato-pasta/#recipe

Equipment

  • Large Stock Pot
  • Small bowl

Ingredients

Prepared Ingredients

  • Dice White Onion - Optional - Small Bowl
  • Diced Basil Leaves - 2 handfuls
  • 5 - 6 Large Garlic Cloves - Minced - Small Bowl

Measured Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon Oregano - Small Bowl
  • 1 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika - Small Bowl
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 14oz cans of Diced Tomatos
  • 4 cups hot water - or room temp, whatever
  • 6oz Tomato Paste
  • 16 oz Penne Pasta
  • 1 cup Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Chilli flakes - Garnish

Instructions

  1. In large stock pot, warm the Olive Oil.
  2. Add Garlic, Paprika, Oregano and Onion. Sauté for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add Tomatoes, Water, Tomato Paste, Salt, and Pepper, and stir.
  4. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium, add the pasta, cover with a lid, and cook until al dente.
  6. Stir every 3-4 minutes and keep an eye on the amount of liquid. If at some point you think it needs more, add more water. 5 cups total might be too much.
  7. When the pasta is still al dente, add the Basil and stir.
  8. Add Ricotta and Parmesan cheese, stir to combine, and serve immediately. Garnish with more Parmesan and Chilli Flakes.
