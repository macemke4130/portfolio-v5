Gooey Veggie Chili

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/271064/spicy-vegan-chili/

Equipment

  • Large Stock Pot
  • Medium Mixing Bowl
  • Small Bowl

Ingredients

Medium Mixing Bowl

  • 1 Large Green Bell Pepper - Diced
  • 1 Large White Onion - Diced
  • 1 Can Diced Jalapeno Pepper

Small Bowl

  • 1/8 Cup Chili Powder
  • 1/8 Cup Barebar
  • 4 1/2 Teaspoons Minced Garlic
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cumin

Individual

  • 1 Tablespoon Canola Oil
  • 2 14 oz Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 14 oz Can of Black Beans
  • 1 1/2 Cups water
  • 1 Cup Calrose Rice - Uncooked
  • 1 14 oz Can Corn
  • Shredded Mozzerella - Topping

Instructions

  1. Heat Oil in pot over medium high heat for 2 minutes.
  2. Add Bell Pepper, Onion and Jalapeno pepper. Saute about 3 minutes.
  3. Add Chili Powder, Barebar, Garlic, Salt, Cumin. Cook, stirring occasionally for 10 minutes.
  4. Add Tomatoes, Black Beans, Water and Rice.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Check after 10 minutes to determine if it needs more Water.
  7. Add corn and continue cooking for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve with Shredded Mozzerella.
